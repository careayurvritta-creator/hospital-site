# Diet Plan for Patients of Fatty Liver Disease

Liver is vital organ of the body and it plays important function in the body. It is very important to keep liver healthy and fit. There are number of liver problems such as liver cirrhosis, fatty liver, hepatitis and many more. There are various causes responsible for liver disorders like too much consumption of alcohol, unhygienic and unhealthy intake of food, which are characterized by symptoms such as paleness, stomach ache, weakness or nausea. One thing is very important, i.e. a balanced diet. A good nutrition is important all the time for the sake of your good health. If you have fatty liver disease then you have to follow a proper diet plan for healthy liver. You should increase intake of daily protein and decrease fats and carbohydrates. You can take 5-6 meals a day.
