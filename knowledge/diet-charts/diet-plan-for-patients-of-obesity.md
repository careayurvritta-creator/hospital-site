Diet Plan for Obesity
Obesity is a condition resulting from the accumulation of excess body fat, which increases the risk of several other Health issues. It is evaluated by body mass Index, if BMI is 30 or higher, then the person is obese. A low-fat, low-carbohydrate, and moderate-protein diet is very helpful in losing excess fat. Obesity causes almost all lifestyle diseases like Diabetes, Heart disease, Arthritis, Cancer, liver problems, infertility, and many more. Balancing Body weight is must to avoid the risk of these diseases, that is only possible with a balanced healthy diet.
DIET INSTRUCTIONS
CEREALS
Cereals to be consumed
Whole Grains, Oatmeal, Quinoa, Barley, Pulses, Brown Rice
Cereals to be avoided
Refined Grains, White Rice, White Refined Flour and its Products
VEGETABLES
Vegetables to be consumed
Green Leafy Vegetables like Spinach, Green Onions, Carrots, Beetroots, Tomatoes, Green Coriander, Turnips, Radish, Okra, Cabbage, Broccoli, Beans, Cauliflower, Mushroom, Zucchini, Pumpkin, Garlic, Cucumber, Ginger, Pumpkin, Bottle Gourd, Ridge / Sponge Gourd, Round Gourd, Ash Gourd, Squash
Vegetables to be avoided
Canned, Dried & Frozen Vegetables, Raw Vegetables, Potatoes, Sweet Potato
PULSES
Pulses to be consumed
Split & Whole Yellow Moong, Green Moong & Pink Lentils, Split Chickpeas, Pigeon Peas, Soybean, and mostly lentils are Good for Obese
Pulses to be avoided
All Other Heavy Lentils like Black Grams, Chickpeas, Kidney Beans, Black Lentils, Horse Gram, Pinto Beans, Garbanzo Beans and Dried or Frozen Pulses
DAIRY PRODUCTS
Dairy Products to be consumed
Skimmed Goat’s & Cow’s Milk in Moderation, Buttermilk (Takra)
Dairy Products to be avoided
Avoid All other Milk & Milk Products
FRUITS
Fruits to be consumed
Black currant, Java Plum, Peach, Apple, Pear, Papaya, Orange, Lemon, Guava, Kiwi, Pomegranate
Fruits to be avoided
Canned, Dried & Frozen Fruits, Mango, Banana, Litchi
SEEDS & DRY FRUITS
Dry Fruits to be consumed
All Soaked Overnight – 5 to 7 Almonds, 1 Walnut, 5 to 6 Raisins, 2 Figs, Flax Seeds, Pumpkin Seeds, Chia Seeds
Dry Fruits to be avoided
Pistachio, Cashew, Sunflower Seeds, Dates, Prunes
SPICES
Spices to be consumed
All in Moderation – Turmeric, Rosemary, Cloves, Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Sesame, Cumin, Carom, Ginger, Coriander, Rock Salt, Black Pepper, Long Pepper, Fenugreek, Mint, Mustard, Parsley
Spices to be avoided
Excess Salt, Red Chili
**Nearly all the Spices are to be Limited. Only Plain and Simple Food should be Preferred.
FATS / OILS
Fats / Oils to be consumed
Cow’s Ghee, Olive Oil, Mustard Oil, Coconut Oil, Canola Oil
Fats / Oils to be avoided
Unsaturated Fats, Refined Oil, Palm Oil, Butter
NON-VEG / FLESH FOODS
Non-Veg / Flesh Foods to be consumed
Egg White, Lean Meat, Skinless Chicken
Non-Veg / Flesh Foods to be avoided
Avoid All Other Non-Vegetarian Food
OTHER FOODS
Other Foods to be consumed
Homemade Food Only, Homemade Soups, Fresh Homemade Green Tea without Lemon, Coconut Water, Herbal Teas like Slimolina Tea, Sprouted Nuts & Grains, Porridge, Fox Nuts, Roasted Grams, Steamed Salad
Other Foods to be avoided
Packaged, Canned, Processed, Fried, Spicy, Junk and Bakery Products, Refined Sugar, Cream-based Liquors, Alcohol, Soft drinks
OTHER TIPS / RECOMMENDATIONS FOR WEIGHT LOSS
** Alterations in the diet is very important as diet plays a 70% role in managing any condition and the rest 30% is done by medication. Moreover, performing some changes in your lifestyle is equally of great help as it also helps in tridosha pacification)
CCF Tea –
Boil ½ tsp each of Fennel, Coriander, Cumin in 3 Cups of water till it’s reduced to 1 cup, Strain and Drink.
Ginger Tulsi Tea –
Boil 1 inch piece of Ginger, 5-6 Basil leaves, and add a pinch of Black Pepper in 2 cups of water till it’s reduced to 1 cup, Strain and Drink.
Juices –
Carrot Juice, Wheatgrass Juice, Bitter Gourd Juice, Aloe Vera Juice, Amla Juice, Beetroot Juice.
3-4 Boiled Indian Gooseberries / Amla
Do not Skip any meal, especially breakfast. Eat Balanced Meals.
Increase Fiber Intake and Consumption of Seasonal Fruits-Vegetables.
Eat a low-calorie and Low-Fat Diet. Increase Physical Activities, Exercise, Yoga.
Must Drink 8- 10 Glasses of Water Daily.
Whole fruit should be preferred to fruit juices.
Food items not mentioned in this diet chart can be consumed in moderation.
In General, all kinds of artificial, packed, industrial, and processed food items should be avoided.
Behavior plays an important role in preventing diseases, so keep yourself away from negative thoughts, anger, greed, and Ego.
Avoid suppression of natural urges such as urination, defecation, sleep, hunger, sneezing etc.
Avoid late-night snacking and eating too late at night. Do not sleep immediately after meals.
Do not sleep for long hours, avoid anger, stress, long travelling, smoking as well as drinking.
Things in this Diet Chart May Vary Patient to Patient due to the Associated Health Issues, so Better to Consult Our Ayurveda Experts for Better Results.
