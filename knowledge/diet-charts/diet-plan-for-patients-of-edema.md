Diet Plan for Patients of Edema
Edema is a medical condition in which puffiness and swelling occurs in many areas of the body, as in legs, arms, feet, hands, lungs, heart, or stomach area. The dietary plan can be very important in the cure and prevention of edema, therefore it is important to make dietary changes to reduce edema
