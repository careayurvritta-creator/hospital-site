Herpes simplex virus is the most common viral infection which is contagious and mainly affects the genitals and oral cavity. It is of two types HSV-1 transmitted through oral contact and HSV-2 which is transmitted sexually and causes oral and genital herpes respectively. The primary symptom of this disease is the appearance of Painful blisters on the infectious site. Patients may also experience tingling, itching, and burning sensation on the affected site. Therefore, the patients need to take a Pitta-shamak Diet, which is without the citrus and spicy edibles, etc. as described below.

## DIET INSTRUCTIONS

Herpes patients require a Pitta-shamak diet that avoids citrus and spicy foods. The diet should focus on cooling and soothing foods that help reduce the inflammatory response associated with herpes outbreaks.

### CEREALS

Wheat, All millets, Rice, Sorghum, Barley are recommended. Refined flour and Rye should be avoided.

### FRUITS

Apple, Muskmelon, Watermelon, Apple, Grapes (in limit), Pomegranate are recommended. All citrus fruits should be avoided.

### VEGETABLES

Pumpkin, Bottle gourd, Coriander, Broccoli, Cauliflower, Carrot, Peas, Round gourd, Asparagus, Potatoes Beans, Drum sticks, Cucumber, Snake gourd are recommended. Capsicum, Brinjal, Tomatoes and all frozen foods should be avoided.

### PULSES

Green gram (Moong daal), Orange lentil (Masur daal) are recommended. Black lentil (Udad daal), Chick pea (Kabuli channa), Kidney beans (Rajmah) should be avoided.

### DAIRY PRODUCTS

Butter milk (Occasionally) is recommended. Whole milk and its products should be avoided.

### SPICES

Turmeric, Coriander, Cumin, Fennel, Green cardamom, Curry leaves, Black pepper are recommended. Chillies, Carom seeds, Fenugreek, Mustard should be avoided.

### DRINKS

Aloevera juice, Coconut water, Herbal tea, Green tea, Pumpkin juice, Aloevera juice, Watermelon juice, Some other leaves water (Tulsi water, Lemongrass water, Mint water, Neem water, Coriander water, Wheatgrass juice) are recommended. Alcohol, Tea, Carbonated and Canned juices and drinks should be avoided.

### SEEDS / NUTS

Almonds (soaked ones), Khus Khus seeds are recommended. Sunflower seeds, Cashew, Pistachios, Flax seeds, Chia seeds, Brazil nuts and peanuts should be avoided.

### OILS / FATS

Cow ghee, Olive oil are recommended. Butter, Sunflower oil, Saturated fats, Refined oils should be avoided.

### NON-VEG

Occasionally can take Egg white and Fish (In limited quantity) is recommended. All red meats and fleshes should be avoided.

### OTHER FOOD

Homemade only, Jaggery, Soups, Tragacanth gum (Gond kateera) are recommended. All processed, Fried food, Junk food, refined sugar, and Oily food and Bakery products should be avoided.

Restrict the intake of Salt, Sugar, Spicy and fermented foods. Increase your water intake.
