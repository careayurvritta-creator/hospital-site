# Diet Chart For Skin Problems

Nutrition is important for health and the best foods for keeping the skin healthy. The skin problem is generally caused by oily food, processed and canned food, refined sugar, and spicy foods which can cause skin problems like acne, cold sore, and blisters. So, treat your skin by eating antioxidant-rich fruit and vegetables, healthy fats from oily fish and nuts as they are rich in antioxidants and fibers. You should take optimal levels of the nutrients that are crucial for radiant skin, including beta carotene, vitamins C and selenium.

## MILK PRODUCTS

### AVOID

Whole Milk and Cream, Full-Fat Yogurt, Condensed Milk, Buttermilk, Curd, Cheese

### ALLOWED TO TAKE

Cow's Ghee, low fat milk

## FRUITS

### AVOID

Coconut, Citrus Fruits, Canned or Packaged Fruits, Tomato & Juices

### ALLOWED TO TAKE

Avocado, Blackcurrant, Blueberries, Guava, Papaya, Apricot, Apple, Indian gooseberry, Pear, Pomegranate, Banana (Day Time, 1 or 2 only) Watermelon, Muskmelon

## VEGETABLES

### AVOID

Canned & Frozen Vegetables, Eggplant, Bell Pepper

### ALLOWED TO TAKE

All Seasonal Vegetables can be consumed

## DRY FRUITS & SEEDS

### AVOID

Pistachio, Cashew Nuts

### ALLOWED TO TAKE

4-5 Almonds, Walnuts, Sunflower Seed, Chia Seed, Flaxseed, Pumpkin Seed (All Soaked in Water Overnight)

## SPICES

### AVOID

Green & Red Chilies, Table Salt (Excess), Black Pepper

### ALLOWED TO TAKE

Cumin, Coriander, Turmeric, Dry Ginger, Fennel, Mint, Thyme, Basil

## OILS

### AVOID

Unsaturated Fats, Palm Oil, Coconut Oil

### ALLOWED TO TAKE

Sunflower Oil, Canola Oil, Olive Oil

## CEREALS

### AVOID

White Refined Flour and its Products

### ALLOWED TO TAKE

Whole Grains, Wheat, Oatmeal, Brown Rice, Flour Ratio: Wheat Flour (80%) + Soybean Flour (10%) + Gram Flour (10%)

## PULSES

### AVOID

Black Gram / Horse Gram, Kidney Beans, Chickpeas, Black Eyed Peas, Black Lentils

### ALLOWED TO TAKE

Yellow and Green Lentils, Split Grams, Pigeon Peas / Tuvar

## NON-VEG / FLESH FOODS

### AVOID

Avoid All other Non-Vegetarian Food

### ALLOWED TO TAKE

Egg white, Grilled Chicken – Once a Week

## OTHERS

### AVOID

Packaged, Processed, Fried and Bakery Products, Honey, Refined Sugar, Jams, Sauces, Pickle

### ALLOWED TO TAKE

Homemade Only, Rose Petals Juice (5-7 Petals), Tofu, Green Juice
