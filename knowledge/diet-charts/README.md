## Features

Each diet chart includes:
1. **Foods to Consume** - Detailed list of recommended foods by category
2. **Foods to Avoid** - Items to avoid for the specific condition
3. **Diet Schedule** - Time-based meal planning
4. **Lifestyle Tips** - Complementary lifestyle recommendations

## Usage

Diet charts are integrated into the AI Diet Chart Generator Tool at `/tools`. The tool:
- Accepts patient health complaints
- Matches against this knowledge base
- Uses AI (Nvidia NIM) to generate personalized diet plans
- References classical Ayurvedic texts (Charaka Samhita, Ashtanga Hridayam)

## Ayurvedic Principles

All diet charts follow classical Ayurvedic principles:
- **Dosha Balance**: Tailored to pacify specific doshas
- **Agni Support**: Foods that enhance digestive fire
- **Seasonal Considerations**: Aligned with Ritucharya
- **Sattvic Quality**: Emphasis on pure, wholesome foods
