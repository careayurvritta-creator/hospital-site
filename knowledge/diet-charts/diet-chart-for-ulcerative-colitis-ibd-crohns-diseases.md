# Diet Chart For Ulcerative Colitis, IBD & Crohn's Diseases

These are inflammatory bowel disorders that involve the mucosa and submucosa of the large intestine. Symptoms of Ulcerative Colitis (UC), IBD, and Crohn's are bleeding, mucous & difficulty in passing stools. Intake of an unbalanced diet and heredity plays a major role to cause UC, IBD, and Crohn's. Low – fat, Low – fiber diet is recommended for IBD patients because it prevents bleeding while passing stool. This diet is also helpful to prevent ailments like diarrhea, Crohn's disease, and cramps. High–fiber diet can increase bowel movements, cramping, and bloating. Food rich in fat can trigger symptoms of ulcerative colitis and it takes a long time for digestion. Avoid raw fruits and vegetables as they are rich in fiber.

## AVOID / ALLOWED

### MILK PRODUCTS

**AVOID:** Milk, Cheese, Tea, Butter, Ice Cream, Lassi and all others

**ALLOWED:** Buttermilk / Takra with Rock Salt, can be consumed, if it suits you. It is preferred to take thick Fresh Curd than Buttermilk. Avoid Sour Curd or Buttermilk.; Thick Fresh Curd / Yogurt; Soy Milk Or Almond Milk – In less quantity; Cow Ghee

### VEGETABLES

**AVOID:** Cucumber, Brinjal, Lady finger, Taro Root, Garlic, Ginger, Leafy Greens (Saag), Capsicum

**ALLOWED:** Potato, Beans, Bottle Gourd, Ridge Gourd, Ash Gourd, Sponge Gourd, Round Gourd, Turnip, Carrot, Spinach, Steamed Asparagus – 50gm Only; Peas, Cauliflower – In Moderation; Mint and Coriander Condiment (Chutney) – In Moderation

### PULSES

**AVOID:** Black Gram, Sorghum, Black Lentils

**ALLOWED:** Split Moong dal with skin removed, All Types of Pulses – Red Lentils, Yellow Lentils, All Types of Rice – Brown Rice, Red Rice, Black Rice

**MODERATE:** Kidney Beans, Black Eyed Beans, Chickpeas, Bengal Gram – In Moderation/Once in a Week

### DRY FRUITS

**AVOID:** Dates, Raisins, Cashew Nuts, Pistachio and Groundnuts

**ALLOWED:** Soaked and Peeled Nuts like Almonds, Walnuts, Dates; Raisins (Only if incomplete evacuation)

### FRUITS

**AVOID:** All Citrus Fruits like Lemon, Lime, Orange, Tomato, Pineapple, Mango, Watermelon, Muskmelon, Sour Pomegranate, Sour Cherries

**ALLOWED:** Bananas can be consumed in more quantity, 4-6 per day; Prefer Steamed Fruits – Apple, Sapodilla Plum, Pear, Ripened Papaya, Sweet Pomegranate, Guava, Avocado (All in Moderate Qty.); Bael fruit, Cherries – Only if Sweet, Sweet Potato (if Suits) (All in Small Qty.)

### OILS

**AVOID:** Refined Oil, Sunflower Oil, Flaxseed Oil, Cod Liver Oil

**ALLOWED:** Mustard Oil, Olive Oil, Rice Bran Oil, Oregano Oil

### SPICES

**AVOID:** Red Chilli, Cloves, Asafetida, Dalchini (Cinnamon), Dry Ginger Powder

**ALLOWED:** Black Pepper, Turmeric, Fresh Curry Leaves, Cumin, Coriander – In Moderation

### NON-VEG / FLESH FOODS

**AVOID:** All Non-Vegetarian Food including Mutton, Beef, Pork, Chicken, Fish, etc.

**ALLOWED:** Only Egg White (limited qty.), Chicken Soup Occasionally

### OTHERS

**AVOID:** All types of Millets, Triphala, Aloe Vera Juice, Pickles, Sweets, Tamarind and Psyllium Husk, Soft/Cold drinks, Junk/Fast foods – Pizza, Pasta, Mayonnaise, Savory, Packaged, Processed, Fried and Bakery Products, Alcohol, Chiwda (Flattened rice), Banana Chips, Potato Chips, Guacamole

**ALLOWED:** Poha, Salty Porridge (Khichdi), Wheat (in Small Qty.), Corn Flour Bread, Plain Dosa with Little Oil, Idli, Uttapam – Non-Spicy; Tragacanth Gum
