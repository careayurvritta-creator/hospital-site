# Diet Chart for GERD (Acid Reflex) - Healthy Food for Acidity

Nutrition is important in the management of gastro-esophageal reflux disease. The therapeutic approach to most GERD problems involves a combination of medication and nutritional therapy. Frequent feedings are important in the case of chronic gastric problems like acidity and GERD. Patients feel more comfortable with fewer spices and bland foods. It is best to avoid a specific food or food habits that trigger acidity.

## Diet Chart for GERD / Acidity

- Aloe Vera juice

### Breakfast

- Veg Semolina (veg upma)
- Veg Vermicelli
- Veg poha (Rice flakes)
- Veg Idli

## Diet Instructions

### Cereals

**To Consume:**
- Whole wheat
- Oatmeal
- Whole grains cereals
- Brown rice

**To Avoid:**
- White refined flour and its products

### Fruits

**To Consume:**
- 5 Almonds (soak in water overnight)
- Watermelon
- Banana
- Musk melon
- Papaya
- Pears
- Apple
- Guava

**To Avoid:**
- Citrus fruits such as Orange, Lemon, Grape fruit, Cranberry

### Vegetables

**To Consume:**
- Asparagus
- Green beans
- Cauliflower
- Pumpkin
- Cabbage
- Broccoli
- Carrots
- Bottle gourd
- Ridge gourd
- Round gourd
- Ash gourd
- Mushroom
- Soya

**To Avoid:**
- Garlic
- Onion
- Capsicum
- Taro root
- Potatoes
- Coconut
- Frozen vegetables

### Pulses

**To Consume:**
- All washed pulses i.e. yellow lentil, Green lentil, Pink lentil, Soybean

**To Avoid:**
- Black gram
- Chickpeas
- Kidney beans

### Dairy Products

**To Consume:**
- Skimmed milk

**To Avoid:**
- Whole milk and cream
- Full-fat yoghurt
- Cream cheese
- Condensed milk

### Spices

**To Consume:**
- Cumin
- Coriander
- Fennel
- Mint
- Fenugreek
- Carmon
- Turmeric

**To Avoid:**
- Red chili
- Green chili
- Cloves
- Black pepper

### Drinks

**To Consume:**
- Coconut water
- Green tea
- Herbal tea
- Aloe Vera juice
- Bitter gourd juice
- Pumpkin juice

**To Avoid:**
- Whole milk drinks
- Cream based liqueurs
- Canned and Packaged soup
- Alcohol
- Sweetened drinks
- Squashes
- Carbonated drinks

### Flesh Foods

**To Consume:**
- None

**To Avoid:**
- All fried foods
- Raw, Uncooked and Processed meat
- Fish especially Selfish, Shark, Swordfish, Tuna
- Burgers, Pork
- Organ meat such as Liver, Kidney, Chest
- Red meat
- Prawns, Lobster, Crab
- Lean beef, Lamb, Ham, Turkey, Rabbit
- Mutton, Duck, Goose

### Oils

**To Consume:**
- Cow ghee
- Canola oil
- Olive oil

**To Avoid:**
- Unsaturated fats
- Cream
- Palm oil
- Coconut oil
- Butter

### Other Foods

**To Consume:**
- Homemade only

**To Avoid:**
- All fried foods
- Junk foods
- Processed foods
- Coconut bar
- Sauces
- Fruit jellies, Jams
- Refined sugar
- Marmalade
- Sweeteners
- Chutneys
- Pickle
- Ice-cream

## Lifestyle and Dietary Tips

- Walk for at least 30-45 min is recommended
- Whole fruit should be preferred to fruit juices
- Skipping of meals should be avoided especially breakfast
- Seasonal fruits and vegetables should be consumed
- Water intake should be increased at least 8-10 glass regularly
- **Herbal Tea** – Boil ½ tsp each of fennel, coriander, cumin in 3 cups of water till it's reduced to 1 cups
- Amla powder - 1 tsp. daily after meals
- Amla (Indian gooseberry) + fennel + licorice powder together 1/2 tsp. daily after meal
- Avoid eating too late at night
- Try to avoid alcohol or limit those foods that cause the most problem
- Do not sleep immediately after meals
- It is advisable to limit the intake of citrus fruits as they can lead to heartburn
