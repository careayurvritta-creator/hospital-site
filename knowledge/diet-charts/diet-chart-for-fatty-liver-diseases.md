In a healthy body, liver helps to remove toxins and produces bile to maintain the digestion. Some amount of fat is required in the liver but when the fat content is too high in the liver the condition is termed as fatty liver. In this condition, the diet that contains a low-fat, less calorie, complex carbohydrates, high-fiber, and protein can reduce the risk of fatty liver disease.

DAILY DIET SCHEDULE

EARLY MORNING
One glass of water + 1 tsp. Fenugreek seeds / Barley water

BREAKFAST
Broken Wheat Porridge (Veg Dalia) / Veg Semolina (Veg Upma) / Veg Vermicelli (Sewian) / Veg Poha (Rice flakes) / Besan Pancake (Gram flour) / Veg Oats / Veg Idli / Stuffed Chapatti / Chapatti with Veg or Dal (1- Egg – white part only occasionally)

MID-MORNING
Fruits / Salad / Coconut water

LUNCH
Plain chapatti / Missi roti / Boiled Rice / Veg / Dal + Curd + Steamed salad
Grilled Fish / Chicken (any one) (once a week)

EVENING
Herbal Tea / Green tea / Roasted Chana + Rice flakes / Sprouts

PRE DINNER
Veg / Chicken Soups (Home-made)

DINNER
Plain chapatti / Missi chapatti / Boiled Rice / Veg + Dal + Steamed salad

DIET INSTRUCTIONS

CEREALS
TO BE CONSUMED: Whole wheat flour, Oats, Whole grains cereals, Porridge, Soybean, Brown rice, Rice flakes, Puffed rice, Millet, Quinoa. Chapatti Ratio – Wheat flour (50%) + Soybean (25%) + Millet (25%)
TO BE AVOIDED: Refine flour and products made from it, Corns.

PULSES
TO BE CONSUMED: All Pulses and Lentils
TO BE AVOIDED: Frozen pulses

FRUITS
TO BE CONSUMED: All fruits
TO BE AVOIDED: Coconut, Mango, Sapodilla, Dates, Banana, All frozen and canned fruits.

VEGETABLES
TO BE CONSUMED: All vegetables including Dark green leafy especially Spinach and Kale, Asparagus, Broccoli, Carrots, Onion, Garlic, Beans, Cabbage, Bottle gourd, Ridge gourd, Round gourd, Bitter gourd, Cauliflower, Bell peppers, Red pepper, Green peas, Potatoes, and Parsley.
TO BE AVOIDED: Yam, Sweet potatoes, Taro roots, Jackfruit, Dried beans, Eggplant, Chili peppers, overcooked or Baked vegetables.

FLESH FOODS
TO BE CONSUMED: Eggs white part only, Roasted or Grilled, Lean meat, cold fish like sardines, salmon and tuna (occasionally in a limited quantity).
TO BE AVOIDED: Fish fried in suitable oil, Prawns, Lobster, Crab, Lean beef, Pork, Lamb, Ham, Very lean minced meat, Turkey, Veal, Rabbit, Egg yolk, Fish roe, Mutton, Duck, Goose, Hot dogs, Meat pies, Organ meat like Liver, Kidney, Chest, Red meat, Smoked and canned fish.

SPICES
TO BE CONSUMED: Fennel, Turmeric, Cumin, Thyme, Black pepper, Coriander, Rock Salt (limited), Cardamom, Cinnamon, Fenugreek.
TO BE AVOIDED: Table Salt (Avoid excessive intake)

DAIRY PRODUCTS
TO BE CONSUMED: Low-fat milk (Occasionally), Curd, Low-fat yogurt.
TO BE AVOIDED: Whole milk and cream, Cream cheese, Condensed milk, Cocoa butter, Full-fat yoghurt, Cottage cheese.

DRYFRUITS AND SEEDS
TO BE CONSUMED: Flax seeds, Chia seeds, Sunflower seeds, Walnuts, Almonds
TO BE AVOIDED: Cashew nuts, Pistachio, Peanuts, Raisins

FATS
TO BE CONSUMED: Olive oil, Canola oil, Cow ghee, Rice bran oil, Soybean oil, Mustard oil
TO BE AVOIDED: Trans fat foods, Butter, Palm oil, Coconut oil, Hydrogenated oil

PREPARED FOODS
TO BE CONSUMED: Homemade only, Jaggery
TO BE AVOIDED: All bakery products or processed, Cakes, Biscuits, Sauces, Fruit jellies, Jams, Mayonnaise, White sugar, Ice-cream, Cream soups, Dark chocolate, Butter scotch, Coconut bar, Chutneys, Pickles, Deep fried foods, All Spicy foods, Junk foods, Too much salty food, Buttered popcorn, Soya sauce.

DRINKS
TO BE CONSUMED: Coconut water, Buttermilk, Home-made soups, Green tea, Beetroot juice, Herbal tea, Aloe Vera juice, Gooseberry juice, Pomegranate juice, Barley water
TO BE AVOIDED: Whole milk drinks, Cream based liqueurs, Canned soup and packaged soup, Alcohol, Sweetened drinks, Squashes, Hot chocolate drinks, Diet soda, Coffee, Tea

HOME REMEDIES

FENUGREEK SEEDS: Soak around one to two teaspoon of fenugreek seeds in water overnight. Strain and drink water in the morning on an empty stomach. Try to chew seeds along with water.

HERBAL TEA: Boil ½ tsp each of fennel, coriander, cumin in 3 cups of water till it's reduced to 1 cup. Drink one or two time in a day.

TULSI GINGER TEA: Boil 1-2 Ginger slices, 4-5 Basil leaves, ½ tsp. of Turmeric in 2 cups of water till it's reduced to 1 cup. Consume this tea once or twice a day.

FLAXSEED: Roasted Flax seed 1 tsp. with normal water, in a day.

DIET TIPS
Walk for at least 30-45 min is recommended.
Whole fruit should be preferred to fruit juices.
Skipping of meals should be avoided, especially the breakfast.
6 almonds soaked per day.
Water intake should be increased at least 8-10 glass regularly.
Cleanliness and hygiene should be maintained.

NOTE: If you are a Diabetic patient then the food high in sugar should be avoided and intake of suitable fats and oils should be strictly limited.
