Diet Chart For Piles / Hemorrhoids

Piles are Dilated or swollen veins around the rectum and anal opening. It is of two types- internal and external. High fiber diet is the ideal diet for persons suffering from piles. Fiber creates bulk in stools and gives relief from hard stools. Avoid spicy, oily food as they trigger gastrointestinal problems and worsen the condition.

**ALLOWED TO TAKE**

#### MILK PRODUCTS

#### MILK PRODUCTS

Whole Milk and Cream, Condensed Milk, Full fat Yogurt, Butter

Cow's Ghee, Low Fat Milk, Buttermilk

#### FRUITS

#### FRUITS

Olives, Citrus Fruits (Lemon, Oranges, etc.) Bananas (it causes constipation)

Apple, Berry (Only ripened), Grapes, Papaya, Pears, Muskmelon, Watermelon

#### VEGETABLES

#### VEGETABLES

Canned & Frozen Vegetables

Green Leafy Vegetables, Spinach, Onion, Cucumber, Carrot, Celery, Beans, Asparagus, Potatoes (Moderate), Sweet Potatoes (Moderate), All Gourd family (Cucumber, Gourd, Bitter Melon, Pumpkin, Squash), Jimikand

#### DRY FRUITS & SEEDS

Nil

4-5 Almonds, Walnuts, Raisins, Sesame Seeds, Sunflower Seed, Chia Seed, Flaxseed, Pumpkin Seed (All Soaked in Water Overnight)

#### SPICES

#### SPICES

Green & Red Chili, Peppers

**Note:** Nearly all the Spices are to be Limited in this Condition. They all can be used only after the Perfect Cure for the Problem. Otherwise, only Plain and Simple Food to be Eaten.

Mint, Turmeric, Cumin, Fennel, Coriander

#### CEREALS

#### CEREALS

Refined Whole Grains, White Refined Flour and its Products

Bran Cereal, Brown Rice with Vegetables, Wheat Pasta (Moderation) with Vegetables, Oats (Simple) with Vegetables, Bagels

**Note:** These Cereals help in the Passage of Stools and managing Other Symptoms as well

#### PULSES

#### PULSES

Black Lentils, Rajma, Chickpea

All Pulses & Legumes

#### OILS

#### OILS

Unsaturated Fats, Palm Oil, Coconut Oil

Sunflower Oil, Canola Oil, Olive Oil, Mustard Oil, Rice Bran Oil, Coconut Oil

#### NON-VEG / FLESH FOODS

#### NON-VEG / FLESH FOODS

Red Meat, Canned & Frozen Meat

**Note:** Meats have a high amount of fat. They are not to be taken during piles

Egg White, Grilled Chicken (Once in 15 Days)

#### OTHERS

#### OTHERS

Packaged, Processed, Fried and Bakery Products, Refined Sugar, Jams, Sauces, Pickle, Too much Salty Food, Coffee, Alcohol, Tea, Dark Chocolate

Homemade Only, Tofu, Honey, Aloe Vera, Wheatgrass

## OTHER TIPS / RECOMMENDATIONS

- Food items not mentioned in this diet chart can be consumed in moderation.
- In General, all kinds of artificial, packed, industrial, and processed food items should be avoided.
- Behavior plays an important role in preventing diseases, so keep yourself away from negative thoughts, anger, greed, and Ego.
- Apply Nirgundi and Jatyadi Oil over Piles after going to Toilet and before Sleeping.
- Keep the Area Clean
