# Diet Chart for Hyperthyroidism

Hyperthyroidism is a condition in which the thyroid gland produces excessive thyroid hormones. The thyroid gland is a butterfly-shaped gland located in front of the neck. This condition speeds up the body's metabolism and can cause weight loss, rapid heartbeat, sweating, irritability, and other symptoms. Proper diet and lifestyle modifications along with medications help in managing hyperthyroidism effectively.

## DIET CHART FOR HYPERTHYROIDISM

### EARLY-MORNING

Amla juice (20 ml) + Warm water (100ml) + 1 tsp Fenugreek seeds water

### BREAKFAST

Broken wheat porridge / Veg Semolina / Veg Idli / Oats / Besan pancake / Missi Roti / Chapatti with Veg + Dal

### MID-MORNING

Seasonal fruit (non-citrus) / Coconut water / Sprouts

### LUNCH

Missi chapatti / Plain chapatti / Vegetable + Dal + Salad / Boiled chicken (occasionally)

### EVENING

Herbal Tea / Roasted Chana / Soup (Home-made)

### DINNER

Missi chapatti / Plain chapatti / Vegetable + Dal / Nutri-nuggets / Salad

## DIET INSTRUCTIONS

### CEREALS

#### Cereals to be consumed

Whole wheat, Whole grains, Brown rice, Oats, Wholewheat bread, Multigrain chapatti, Barley, Quinoa

**Flour Ratio** – Chana Flour – 250 gm + Wheat flour – 1 kg

#### Cereals to be avoided

Refined flour and its products, White rice, Cornflakes, Pasta, Pizza, White bread

### FRUITS

#### Fruits to be consumed

Apple, Pear, Papaya, Melon, Guava, Pomegranate, Grapes, Peach, Apricot, Plum, Berries, Figs, Prunes

#### Fruits to be avoided

Orange, Lime, Lemon, Grapefruit, Kiwi, Banana (excess), Canned fruit, Frozen fruit

### VEGETABLES

#### Vegetables to be consumed

Bottle gourd, Ridge gourd, Round gourd, Cabbage, Cauliflower, Broccoli, Tomatoes, Celery, Kale, Carrot, Capsicum, Cucumber, Pumpkin, Peas, Beans, Green beans, Fenugreek leaves, Turnip, Radish, Beetroot, Mushrooms, Artichoke

#### Vegetables to be avoided

Spinach (excess), Bitter gourd (excess), Frozen or Canned vegetables, Potato, Sweet potato, Yam, Jackfruit

### PULSES

#### Pulses to be consumed

All split lentils and Legumes, Chickpeas, Kidney beans, Soya beans

**Note:** Soak all pulses overnight and pressure cook for better digestion

#### Pulses to be avoided

Fried pulses, Frozen pulses

### DAIRY PRODUCTS

#### Dairy Products to be consumed

Buttermilk, Home-made yogurt (low-fat), Tofu, Cottage cheese (paneer)

#### Dairy Products to be avoided

Full-fat milk, Cream, Butter, Cheese, Condensed milk, Full-fat Yogurt, Ice-cream

### SPICES

#### Spices to be consumed

Cumin, Coriander, Turmeric, Ginger, Pepper, Fennel, Cinnamon, Cardamom, Fenugreek, Turmeric

#### Spices to be avoided

Excess salt, Garlic (excess), Onion (excess)

### DRINKS

#### Drinks to to be consumed

Coconut water, Pomegranate juice, Apple juice, Herbal tea, Green tea, Water

#### Drinks to be avoided

Caffeine (coffee, tea), Alcohol, Carbonated beverages, Packaged fruit juices, Cold drinks, Sports drinks

### FLESH FOODS

#### Flesh Foods to be consumed

Egg white, Roasted or Grilled Fish (salmon, tuna), Chicken breast, Turkey breast

#### Flesh Foods to be avoided

Red meat, Beef, Pork, Lamb, Ham, Duck, Goose, Organ parts, Prawns, Lobster, Crab, Mutton, Egg yolk, Oily fish

### SEEDS AND DRY FRUITS

#### Dry Fruits to be consumed

Almonds (soaked), Walnuts, Pumpkin Seeds, Sunflower Seeds

#### Dry Fruits to be avoided

Pistachio, Cashew, Raisins, Salted nuts, Brazil nuts

### OILS

#### Oils to be consumed

Olive oil, Sunflower oil, Mustard oil, Canola oil, Sesame oil 500 ml

**Note:** 500ml of cooking oil to be used per month

#### Oils to be avoided

Trans-fat, Hydrogenated oil, Vanaspati ghee, Coconut oil (excess)

### OTHER FOODS

#### Other Foods to be consumed

Honey (limited), Jaggery (limited)

#### Other Foods to be avoided

All bakery products, Processed foods, Fast food, Fried foods, Chocolate, Candy, Refined sugar, Artificial sweetener, Soy products, Seaweed (excess iodine), Iodine supplements

## LIFESTYLE AND DIETARY TIPS

- Walk for at least 30-45 min daily (light exercise).
- Yoga and Meditation is recommended.
- Drink at least 8-10 glasses of water daily.
- **Herbal Tea** – Boil ½ tsp each of fennel, coriander, cumin in 3 cup of water till it's reduced to 1 cup.
- **Amla juice** – 20ml with water empty stomach.
- Eat smaller, more frequent meals.
- Avoid high-energy foods that may increase metabolism further.
- Get adequate rest and sleep.
- Manage stress through meditation or breathing exercises.
- Avoid excessive physical exertion.
- Regular thyroid function tests.
