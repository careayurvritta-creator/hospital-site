## Vegetables

**To Consume:** Bottle gourd, Ridge gourd, Spinach, Pointed gourd, Bitter gourd

**To Avoid:** Garlic, Ginger, Tomato, Brinjal, Spinach

## Fruits

**To Consume:** Banana, Pomegranate, Grapefruit, Pear

**To Avoid:** Orange, Sweet lime, Fruits with seeds, Pineapple, Lemon

## Pulses

**To Consume:** Green gram, Other lentils based on disease severity, Mung dal, Toor dal, Chana dal, Masur dal

**To Avoid:** Chana (Kabuli), Rajma, Black gram (Udad dal)

## Spices

**To Consume:** Turmeric, Asafoetida, Cumin, Coriander, Fennel

**To Avoid:** All types

Note: All in small amounts

## Other Foods

**To Consume:** Egg white (only the white part), Water, Fresh yogurt, Coconut water, Marigold flower juice, Rose petal juice, Mung dal khichdi, Green gram dal curry, Buttermilk, Vitamins, Proteins, Fats, Minerals

**To Avoid:** Ice cream, Fast food, Milk, Tea, Coffee, Soft drinks, Maggie, Peanuts, Pasta, Milk products, Bread, Oats, Sauces, Spicy foods, Fried foods
